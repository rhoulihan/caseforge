Hi team,

Following up on Project Atlas. Here's the current shape of our production MongoDB
deployment at Northwind Mutual Insurance so you can size the move to Oracle Autonomous
Database.

- The cluster has 3 shards (data-bearing replica sets).
- Each data-bearing node in the primary region has 32 vCPU.
- Our DR region nodes are smaller — 16 vCPU each.
- On-disk (compressed) data is roughly 2 TB and growing ~5% a quarter.
- Primary cluster CPU runs fairly low on average but spikes hard at month-end close.

The main production primary is db-prod-01.nw.local (10.20.30.40). DR replicates to
db-dr-01.nw.local in our second data center.
